# Contributing

This skill improves when people contribute realistic decisions, failure cases, and examples.

Good contributions include:

- consequential decisions with real trade-offs
- examples where the council converged too quickly, missed a decisive variable, or flattened disagreement
- eval prompts with clear pass/fail expectations
- small wording improvements that preserve independent reasoning and useful dissent

Please keep contributions focused on the skill's behavior. The goal is better judgment infrastructure, not more documentation for its own sake.

Open an issue with:

1. the decision context
2. what the council should surface
3. what failure mode you want it to avoid
